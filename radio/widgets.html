Widgets & Integrations

Want to feature your favorite Kenyan station on your own website or blog? Kenya Radio Network provides easy-to-use widgets that allow your visitors to listen without leaving your site.

Embeddable Player Widget (Standard)

Copy the following HTML code snippet and paste it into the body of your website. This will display a small player for the station of your choice. (Note: Replace `[STATION_ID]` with the unique ID of the station you want to feature.) 

<iframe 
  src="https://yourdomain.com/widget.html?station=[STATION_ID]" 
  width="300" 
  height="150" 
  style="border:none;">
</iframe>

**Customization:** You can adjust the `width` and `height` parameters to fit your layout.

Direct Stream Link

If you want to link directly to a station's stream for use in external media players (like VLC), please contact us. We can provide the direct M3U8 or MP3 URL for authenticated use, subject to the station's permissions.
